Next Generation of Apache 250 is Releasing soon

TVS Apache RTR 160 is a commuter bike available at a starting price of Rs. 1,02,272 in India. It is available in 2 variants and 6 colours with top variant price starting from Rs. 1,05,274. The Apache RTR 160 is powered by 159.7cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 15.1 bhp and a torque of 13.9 Nm. With front disc and rear drum brakes, TVS Apache RTR 160 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This Apache RTR 160 bike weighs 139 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 12 liters.
